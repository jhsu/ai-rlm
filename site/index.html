How it works

Turn one oversized prompt into inspectable steps.

Instead of asking a model to solve everything in one pass, AI-RLM lets your workflow plan smaller calls, execute them, and preserve the trail of intermediate reasoning your team needs to debug and improve it. 

const result = await rlm({
  model,
  prompt: "Compare these documents and explain the risks",
  tools: {
    searchDocs,
    runAnalysis,
  },
});

console.log(result.answer);
console.log(result.steps);

For technical leaders

Give your team a reusable pattern for serious AI workflows.

AI-RLM is not a black-box agent platform. It is a developer-controlled library for teams that need recursive reasoning, inspectable execution, and room to adapt as models, tools, and product requirements change.
